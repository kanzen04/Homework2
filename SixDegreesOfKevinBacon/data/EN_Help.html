Six Degrees of Kevin Bacon (Oscars Edition)

Six Degrees of Kevin Bacon is a fun game where one tries to connect various actors to Kevin Bacon through the films they've been in with other actors. In this edition we'll only use actors who have won Academy Awards, either for Best Actor, Best Actress, Best Supporting Actor, or Best Supporting Actress.

During gameplay, when a game starts, the player is given a random actor and from that individual one must find a path back to Kevin Bacon. Note that there is a path for all actors included in the game. One may not revisit nodes in the path, so one may not use a Film or Actor more than once. The player will be notified of dead ends, like when one chooses a film that doesn't have any other Academy Award-winning actors other than the one that brought you there.

The Six Degrees of Kevin Bacon book was published in 1996.
